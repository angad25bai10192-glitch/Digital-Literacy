# Stay Safe Online Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Always verify unknown calls, messages, or emails before responding  
- Never share personal information like OTPs or passwords  
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files  
- Regularly update apps and devices for security patches  

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers  
- Double-check payment requests before approving transactions  
- Use official banking apps only  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
